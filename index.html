    SGTU - An Overview

    Shree Guru Gobind Singh Tricentenary University, Gurugram is one of the best private universities in Delhi NCR. It has a noble mission of providing opportunities for higher education to all sections of society. The University over time has carved a reputation in the academic world by providing a steady stream of highly trained and industry-ready professionals. SGT University is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC). Apart from these, the University is also recognised by Haryana Government.

      Approval and Accreditation

    • The University also has a dedicated multi-speciality SGT Hospital with NABL & NABH accreditation.
    • SGT University is UGC approved and has the necessary approvals from Haryana Government and regulatory approvals from key government bodies.

      Ranking

    • SGT University, besides being in the top private universities in India, is also highly rated.
    • SGT has received several awards including the Diamond rating from the QS-IGAUGE ranking.
    • The University boasts of having Asia’s one and only National Reference Simulation Centre for Nursing & Medical Science.

    Eligibility criteria

    Undergraduate

    B.E

    Passed the 2nd PUC/ 12th / Equivalent Exam with English as one of the languages with a minimum of 45% of marks in aggregate in Physics and Mathematics along with Chemistry/ Biotechnology / Biology / Electronics/ Computers. (40% for Karnataka reserved category candidates).

    Taking any one entrance exam is Mandatory. CET/ COMED-K / JEE / AIEEE

    Post-Graduate

    M.Tech (Technology/CSE/VLSI Design & Embedded Systems)

    Relevant BE/BTech degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate (45% for Karnataka SC/ST category) from a recognized university.

    Entrance Exam (anyone): PGCET/GATE in core specialisation

    Master of Computer Application (MCA)

    Relevant Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate (45% for Karnataka SC/ST category) from a recognized university.

    Entrance Exam: PGCET/KMAT

    Campus life

    Hostel: The SGT University features separate dorms for girls and boys, each having its own dining halls, entertainment rooms, study rooms, and round-the-clock security. The SGT University hostel rooms are equipped with the required amenities.

    Library: The SGT University library is completely air-conditioned with internet access for students and a collection of national and international journals.

    Classrooms: The classrooms are large, well-lit, and equipped with the latest multimedia teaching aids. Wi-Fi is also available across the campus.

    Transportation: The SGT University operates a fleet of around 15 buses that serve students and employees on different routes throughout the NCR and its surrounding areas.

    Seminar Hall: The seminar hall is ideal for seminars and medical professionals to speak and deliver lectures. These conferences and lectures provide students with first-hand information on the workings of many fields.
